About Me

Software Engineer - Graphic Designer - Transcriber

I am a Software Engineer with a strong foundation in coding, problem-solving, and web development from Moringa School. Currently, I’m wrapping up my academic journey in software engineering, where I’ve gained a deep understanding of programming languages (HTML, CSS, JS/REACT, PYTHON/FLASK), software design principles, and real-world application development. My passion for technology drives me to continuously learn and apply innovative solutions to complex challenges. Before transitioning into software engineering, I have been working a System Administrator for a busy company here in Kenya. I am also a Graphic Designer, with several years of experience in the design industry, I have honed my skills in visual communication, branding, and digital design. My expertise spans across various design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
